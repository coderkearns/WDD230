Carter Kearns

Hick's Law

Lemon

https://lemon.io/

Even though there is a large amount of content on the page, all the buttons are the same action. The only choice the reader need make is whether to click the button or not.

Visual Hierarchy

Express.js

https://expressjs.com/

The main title (express) is bold and there first place your eyes go. Next they follow down to the large but slightly transparent subheading. Below, each section has a clear title and content. At the very bottom, all the "uninportant" links and information is very small and no effort is really made to create distinction between the links to different liscences.

Alignment

Evernote

https://evernote.com/

The titles, paragraphs, images, and buttons are all center-aligned so you don't need to switch between types of reading.
